Centaur Finance

What is Centaur Finance?

Centaur Finance: Bridging DeFi and traditional finance

Mainnet

What is its purpose?

The mainnet will be used to offer DeFi related solutions from FinTech partners. It is designed to serve as a settlement layer that aggregates data across the various liquidity pools and is intended to assist with cross-chain compatibility.

The mainnet is powered by the Tendermint Core Consensus and delivers a high throughput blockchain with ease of integration for developers.

Centaur Swap

What is Centaur Swap?

Centaur Swap is designed to offer single-side staking for liquidity providers. It uses oracle based liquidity pools to prevent impermanant loss that is inherent with paired liquidity pools as those found on Uniswap and Pancakeswap.

Centaur Swap is currently in its initial release and is undergoing both technical and economics audits. Future releases will see L2 onboarding as well as the integration of more tokens.

What is WHEY?

The primary use case for WHEY is governance. For example, governance would include decisions on fee rates and new tokens to list.

Hadar

What is Hadar?

Hadar is a multi-asset, multi-address, multi-purpose wallet. It is an all-in-one app for crypto-asset storage, management and decentralised finance.

When will Hadar be released

Hadar is available on the Playstore. The iOS version is under review by Apple.
